Works & Projects

PRD & Business Plan : COLORMOOD —  Capturing the Hues of Existence

In an era of information overload, ColorMood serves as a visual diary that bridges the gap between abstract feelings and aesthetic clarity. With its unique "One Photo, One Palette" philosophy, it transforms everyday randomness into a curated museum of personal moods, helping users find their own "base color" and connect with those who resonate on the same frequency.

Business Plan : PurrCircle — Community-Based Pet Mutual Assistance  

TOGETHER WITH Yasi YANG*, Yixuan ZHOU, Wanru YAN in course IDE2000
PurrCircle is a hyper-local pet care platform that solves the "travel anxiety" of 80 million urban pet owners in China. By activating community trust through a dual-track model, it connects owners with verified neighborhood helpers for affordable, in-home care.

Project: Dynamic Gesture Recognition：A Novel Approach for CAPTCHA  

TOGETHER WITH Zhiyuan YANG, Shengxi Xiong
A next-generation authentication system that replaces traditional methods with real-time hand gesture recognition. Powered by a custom CNN with Residual and SE blocks, the system achieved 99.7% accuracy across 13,000+ self-made samples, successfully bridging high-performance Computer Vision with frictionless user cybersecurity.

Publication: An Optimized Port Operation Efficiency Prediction Model Based on LSTM and ESN  

Xinxin Liu, Ruixiang Mei, Ziqi Zhao , Shaohan Wang, Junli Duan*
This paper introduces a high-precision dual-model framework to predict smart port operation efficiency, addressing the challenges of nonlinear dynamics and noisy multi-scale data. By integrating CEEMDAN for signal decomposition with a lightweight Echo State Network (ESN) and a Bidirectional Attention-Enhanced LSTM (Bi-ALSTM), the system achieves a significant leap in temporal modeling. Experimental results demonstrate a 30.5% reduction in MSE compared to traditional RNNs, while maintaining a real-time training speed of just 0.35 seconds, providing a robust quantitative tool for dynamic scheduling and risk early warning in global supply chains.
